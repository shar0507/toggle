SocialConnect

A social networking platform that allows users to connect and share content with friends and family. It provides a user-friendly interface, real-time messaging, and a customizable feed to keep users engaged and connected.

Technologies: HTML, CSS, Flask

Rapid

Developed a news application in Java, with the aim of providing the latest updates on happenings across the country and around the world. Serves as a platform for users to read quick highlights on various topics of interest- politics, sports, entertainment, technology and more.

Technologies: XML, Java

TicketChain

A secure and transparent ticketing platform based on blockchain technology, ensuring the authenticity of bus tickets. It enables users to purchase, sell, and verify bus tickets securely, reducing the possibility of fraud and ensuring a transparent ticketing system.

Technologies: Blockchain, Solidity

BusTracker IoT

An IoT-based system designed to provide real-time tracking and management of bus fleets. It includes GPS modules installed in buses, enabling users to track bus locations and estimated arrival times in real-time through a mobile application. The system also monitors key parameters such as engine health and fuel consumption to ensure efficient fleet management and maintenance scheduling.

Technologies: Rpi, IOT

IoT-based Smart Segregation System

A waste segregation system equipped with several sensors capable of identifying and separating dry, wet and metallic waste.

Technologies: Arduino
